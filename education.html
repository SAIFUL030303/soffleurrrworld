My education journey began with me attending Sekolah Kebangsaan Bandar Baru Sungai Buloh, Selangor (SKBBSB) under the Gombak district from grade 1 to grade 6 which is at the UPSR level.

After being at SKBBSB, I went to secondary school at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Bandar Baru Sungai Buloh, Selangor, where my SMK school was just next to SK. I went to SMK from Form 1 to Form 5, which is up to SPM level.
